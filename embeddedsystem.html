Our Projects and Features

Smart HVAC Controller
  • Climate Monitoring: Measure indoor temperature, humidity, and air quality.
  • Automated Control: Adjust HVAC settings based on environmental data and user preferences.
Automated Inventory Management System
  • RFID Scanning: Use scanners to track items entering or leaving inventory.
  • Real-Time Tracking: Monitor stock levels and update inventory counts automatically.
Smart Lighting Control System
  • Automated Scheduling: Set lighting schedules based on time of day or occupancy.
  • Remote Control: Enable control via a mobile app or centralized system.
